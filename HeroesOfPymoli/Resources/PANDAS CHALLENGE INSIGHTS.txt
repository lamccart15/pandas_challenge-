PANDAS CHALLENGE – HEROES OF PYMOLI – INSIGHTS
Lauren McCarthy 
March 24th, 2021

The following insights can be drawn from the analysis of the provided purchasing data: 
- The majority of players are male. 
- The majority of players are within the ages of 15 to 29. 
- The most profitable age group is 20-24. 
- The “Final Critic”, the “Oathbreaker, Last Hope of the Breaking Storm”, and the “Fiery Glass Crusader” are amongst the most popular and most profitable items in the game.
